Meet Sky Wizard

A magical weather app with interactive 3D models, animated forecasts, and a wizard guide for real-time updates. Weather has never been this enchanting!

View on AppStore

View daily and hourly weather updates

Stay informed with precise and up-to-date weather forecasts. Check daily conditions or plan your activities with detailed hourly updates, ensuring you're never caught off guard by sudden weather changes.

Animated weather icons

Experience the weather come alive with beautifully animated icons that depict current conditions vividly. From sunny skies to thunderstorms, these animations add a touch of life to your weather updates.

Interact with the 3D house

Dive into an immersive experience by exploring the interactive 3D house in the app. Engage with different parts of the house to reveal unique weather-related surprises and insights

Get forecasts from the wizard inside the app

Meet your personal weather guide, the Sky Wizard. This mystical character delivers your weather forecasts with a touch of whimsy and charm, making each update both informative and delightful

It's open source

Sky Wizard is built with transparency and collaboration in mind. As an open-source project, the app's code is freely available for developers and enthusiasts to explore, contribute, or customize. Join the community and be part of shaping the future of Sky Wizard by accessing the source code on GitHub.
