Sky Wizard Privacy Policy

1. Ownership

The iOS application Sky Wizard is created by Hishara Weerasinghe and published by Stephen Innovations via its developer account on the App Store.

2. Information Collected

The only information collected by Sky Wizard is the user’s approximate location. This data is accessed through the device’s GPS hardware to provide relevant weather conditions. The app does not collect or access the user’s precise location.

3. Use of Collected Information

The collected location data is not stored or saved by Sky Wizard. It is solely used to determine the current weather conditions and to obtain the city name through reverse geocoding.

4. Sharing of Information

Sky Wizard guarantees that no collected information will be shared or sold to third parties. The app does not collect or transmit any personal information such as names, addresses, or phone numbers.

5. User Rights

Users have the right to know which services the app uses to obtain the city name and weather information. This information is detailed in the “About Application” screen within the app, including the sources of data.

6. Security Measures

The collected location data is sent to the data source APIs (weather, city name) through HTTPS, which uses encryption. Therefore, the data is encrypted during network transmission and only decrypted once received.

7. Data Retention

The location data is accessed by the system APIs (CoreLocation). The app does not retain any data after accessing it.

8. Third-Party Libraries

The app does not use any third-party libraries that collect or analyze user data.

9. Children's Privacy

The app is not designed specifically for children. However, there is no harmful content in the app for children.

10. Updates to the Privacy Policy

Any future updates to this privacy policy will be posted on the existing web URL.

11. Contact Information

For any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact Hishara Weerasinghe at hishara@gmail.com.

Sky Wizard is committed to user privacy and transparency. Thank you for trusting us with your weather updates.
